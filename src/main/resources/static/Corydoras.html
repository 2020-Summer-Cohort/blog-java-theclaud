The Corydora Sterbai

The Sterba's Cory also known as the Corydora Sterbai, are amongst the most popular freshwater fish. These bottom dwellers are are fun and social, best kept with other cory's. They have an attractive pattern, white spots on a brown body. Catch them hanging out under drift wood and caves. Considered a sucker fish as they stick to the side of the glass. Easy to care for and spectacular addition to your tank.
